Hello, Pizza Lovers!

Our History

At Jenny's Za, we pride ourselves on crafting pizzas that are nothing short of extraordinary. Our team of passionate pizza artisans meticulously handcrafts each pie, using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques. From the crispy, golden crust to the mouthwatering toppings and delectable sauces, every bite is a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more.

Our app is designed to make your pizza ordering experience effortless and enjoyable. With a user-friendly interface, you can easily explore our diverse menu of signature pizzas, each one thoughtfully curated to offer a unique and unforgettable taste. Whether you're a fan of classic combinations or adventurous flavor profiles, Jenny's Za has something to satisfy every palate.

Our Ingredients

We believe that the key to exceptional pizza lies in the quality and selection of ingredients. We meticulously source the finest and freshest ingredients to ensure that every bite of our pizza is a culinary delight.

Farm-Fresh Produce: We partner with local farmers who share our passion for quality and sustainability. Our vegetables and herbs are grown with care, using organic and eco-friendly practices. From vibrant tomatoes and crisp bell peppers to fragrant basil and earthy mushrooms, our pizzas are bursting with the flavors of the season.

Ethically Raised Meats: We source our meats from responsible and ethical farms, ensuring that our pizzas feature the finest quality proteins. Whether it's savory pepperoni, juicy grilled chicken, or tender Italian sausage, you can indulge in our delicious meat toppings with the confidence that they come from animals raised with care and respect.

Farm to Table



Crust: Our pizza starts with a crust that is the perfect balance of crispy and chewy. Handcrafted with care, our crust is made using premium flour, yeast, and a touch of our secret ingredient for that extra hint of flavor.

Sauces: Our selection of sauces is a celebration of diverse flavors. From tangy tomato sauce to creamy garlic parmesan, each sauce is carefully crafted to complement the toppings and enhance the overall taste experience.

Cheeses: We believe in the power of cheese to elevate a pizza to new heights. That's why we offer a variety of artisanal cheeses, including gooey mozzarella, sharp cheddar, creamy goat cheese, and velvety ricotta. These cheeses are carefully selected for their quality and flavor, ensuring a cheesy delight in every bite.

Toppings: Our toppings are a celebration of both classic favorites and unique, adventurous flavors. We source fresh vegetables, premium meats, and flavorful herbs to create a wide array of topping options. Whether you're craving traditional pepperoni, smoky bacon, vibrant bell peppers, or exotic ingredients like artichokes or truffle oil, we have a topping combination to satisfy every craving.

Herbs and Spices: To add that extra dash of flavor, we sprinkle our pizzas with a blend of aromatic herbs and spices. From oregano and basil to red pepper flakes and garlic powder, these seasonings enhance the overall taste profile and create a memorable pizza experience.

Specialty Pizzas: In addition to our build-your-own options, we offer a selection of specialty pizzas that showcase unique flavor combinations crafted by our pizza artisans. These pizzas are inspired by global cuisines, local favorites, and innovative culinary trends, ensuring that there's always something exciting to explore and indulge in.
